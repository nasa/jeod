std::string jeod::MassPointInit::name {""}

Item name, with the following semantics for items that have a name:

  • The mass point name will always be of the form "mass_name.point_name".
  • If the supplied name does not begin with "mass_name.", this prefix will be applied to the supplied name in naming the mass point.
