72 /**
73  * A constraint frame is directly connected to a vehicle structural frame, which
74  * in turn is connected to a root vehicle structural frame.
75  * A ConstraintFrame object contains both the connection to the immediate
76  * structural frame and to the root vehicle structural frame.
77  */
