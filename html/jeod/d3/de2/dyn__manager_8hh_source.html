96 /**
97  * The DynManager class manages the dynamic elements of a simulation.
98  * The primary functions of a DynManager are to:
99  * - Dynamically determine which ephemerides are needed in a simulation.
100  * - Initialize ephemeris models and keep them in sync with the rest
101  * of the simulation.
102  * - Initialize mass bodies and dynamic bodies independently of the order
103  * in which these bodies are declared in the S_define file.
104  * - Coordinate the computation of the cumulative forces and torques and
105  * gravitational effects on the dynamic bodies in a simulation.
106  * - Coordinate the integration of time and and of dynamic body states.
107  * - Apply asynchronous actions to bodies.
108  *
109  * The DynManager can operate in one of three modes: empty space,
110  * single planet, and ephemeris mode. The DynManager inherits from
111  * EphemerisInterface so that when it operates in empty space or
112  * single-planet mode it can properly register itself as
113  * the owner of the reference frame tree root node.
114  */

117  virtual public BaseDynManager,
