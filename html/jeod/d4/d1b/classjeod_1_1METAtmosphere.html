◆ fairing_k

const double jeod::METAtmosphere::fairing_k
private

trick_units(rad/km) Factor which, when multiplied by the altitude delta above the base-fairing-height provides an angle. The square of the cosine of that angle indicates how much of the seasonal-variation in Helium density to apply. density = corrected-density * (non-corrected-density / corrected-density) ^ (cos^2 ( fairing_k * delta-altitude)) At base-fairing-height, none gets applied. By 500km, it all gets applied.
