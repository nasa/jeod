Apply both sets of coefficients to the supplied history data.

The first element of the output state_sum is calculated as the inner products of the acceleration history with the summed Adams coefficients. The second element is calculated as the inner product with the Gauss-Jackson coefficients. (First = first integral; second = second integral.)

Parameters

ncoeffNumber of elements in the acceleration history
