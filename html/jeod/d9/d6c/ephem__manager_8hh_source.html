81  * The EphemeridesManager class manages the ephemeris models in a simulation.
82  * The primary functions of a EphemeridesManager are to:
83  * - Maintain lists of planets, ephemeris models, ephemeris items, and
84  * integration frames, and to provide lookup methods for these lists.
85  * - Dynamically determine which ephemerides are needed in a simulation.
86  * - Initialize ephemeris models and keep them in sync with the rest
