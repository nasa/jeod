82 /**
83  * Provides the basic ability to attach one MassBody to another.
84  * This can be either an initialization or asynchronous BodyAction.
85  * The action will be performed when the sim user or some simulation job
86  * enables the active flag.
87  *
88  * MassBodyAttach actions that are ready at simulation initialization time
89  * are run as a part of the initialization process, sandwiched between
90  * initializing mass properties and initializing state. Attach actions
91  * that are not ready at initialization time remain in the pending actions
92  * queue until the active flag is set.
93  */
