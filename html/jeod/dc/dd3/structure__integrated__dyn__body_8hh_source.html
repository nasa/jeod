72 /**
73  * Extends DynBody to integrate an object's structural reference frame
74  * as opposed to its center of mass.
75  *
76  * In addition to structure integration, this class introduces two new concepts,
77  * wrenches and constrained objects. A wrench encapsulates a force applied at a
78  * point and a torque, with the torque induced by the force due to an
79  * off-centerline force direction automatically calculated by JEOD.
80  * A constrained object is an object that lies outside the DynBody system
81  * boundary that exchanges translational and/or rotational momentum with
82  * the DynBody and that is somehow constrained by the translation and/or
83  * rotational behavior of the DynBody.
84  *
85  * These new concepts might be migrated up the DynBody inheritance chain in
86  * subsequent releases of JEOD.
87  */
88 class StructureIntegratedDynBody : public DynBody
