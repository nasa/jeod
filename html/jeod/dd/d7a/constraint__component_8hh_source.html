71 /**
72  * Represents a single scalar constraint, either a pure force in a specific
73  * direction at a specific point or a pure torque in a specific direction,
74  * that acts on some vehicle.
75 
76  * The force or torque on the vehicle is some scalar value times the direction
77  * in which the force/torque acts. The force/torque the vehicle exerts on
78  * the constraint body in this direction is the additive inverse of the
79  * force/torque the constraint body exerts the vehicle. The behavior of the
80  * constraint body can also be described as some function of the total forces
81  * and torques on the vehicle (external and constraints), plus inertial effects.
82  * This leads to an equation for the scalar constraint force/torque value for
83  * each constraint.
84  *
85  * The class DynBodyConstraintsSolver builds and then solves a system of
86  * equations for all of the constrained bodies. This yields values for each
87  * of the constraints, and hence for the total constraint forces and torques
88  * on the vehicle.
89  */
