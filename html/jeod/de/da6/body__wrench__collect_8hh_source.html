67 /**
68  * Serves as the collection point for wrenches that act on a vehicle.
69  * This is a simple class that is tightly coupled with the
70  * StructureIntegratedDynBody class. This latter class contains (has-a)
71  * a BodyWrenchCollect data member.
72  *
73  * The Trick vcollect mechanism (or a similar mechanism in a non-Trick sim)
74  * pushes pointers to the individual wrenches onto the various collection
75  * member of a BodyWrenchCollect. StructureIntegratedDynBody members cumulate
76  * these collected wrenches to form the total wrench acting on the vehicle.
77  */
